What to Expect

Your experience at Luna & Sage Studio is crafted from start to finish.

💭

Consultation

We begin every appointment with a thorough consultation. We listen to your ideas, assess your hair, and discuss what is achievable. No appointment starts without us being on the same page.

✨

The Experience

Sit back and enjoy the process. Our studio is designed for relaxation with comfortable seating, good music, and products that smell as good as they feel. We take our time so you leave feeling renewed.

🧴

Aftercare

Before you leave, we walk you through how to maintain your look at home. We recommend products suited to your hair type and show you styling techniques so your results last as long as possible.

Our Studio

A space designed for creativity and comfort.

Luna & Sage Studio is located in the heart of Portland, housed in a light-filled space with exposed brick, warm wood tones, and curated greenery. Every station is set up for both stylist comfort and client relaxation.

We use professional-grade products from Oribe, Davines, and KEVIN.MURPHY, chosen for their performance, ingredient quality, and commitment to sustainability. Our commitment extends to eco-friendly practices including low-waste color services and recyclable packaging.

Whether you are visiting for a quick trim or a full transformation, you will find a salon that values your time, your vision, and your trust.
